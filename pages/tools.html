🧱 Geography 20-Marker Answer Planner

Goal: Structure and write a high-quality 20-mark answer with good AO1, AO2, and AO3 balance in under 25 minutes.

🧠 Step 1: Understand the Command Word (1 min)

  • Highlight what it's asking you to do: evaluate, discuss, to what extent, assess
  • Re-write the question in your own words

🧱 Step 2: Plan Your Structure (3–4 min)

  • Introduction: Brief context + define key terms
  • Main Point 1: AO1 + explanation + case study + mini conclusion
  • Main Point 2: AO1 + explanation + contrasting case/example + evaluation
  • Main Point 3 (Optional): AO2/interaction or human/physical link
  • Conclusion: Return to question + weigh up overall judgement

⏳ Suggested Time Breakdown (25 min total)

  • Plan – 4 min
  • Write each section – 18 min
  • Quick check/edit – 2–3 min

📌 Key Reminders

  • Always include at least 2 named examples/case studies
  • Use connectives: "This suggests…" "However…" "On the other hand…"
  • Link each paragraph clearly back to the question (mini-conclusions)
  • Don't waffle. Prioritise clarity, evaluation, and application

✅ Checklist (tick as you go)

  • [ ] Rephrased the question
  • [ ] Wrote a clear introduction
  • [ ] Included 2+ detailed examples
  • [ ] Made a judgement in each paragraph
  • [ ] Wrote a conclusion that answers the question
  • [ ] Proofread for links and balance

🧱 Geography 9-Marker Answer Planner

Goal: Build a strong, structured answer to 9-mark questions using 3 clear points and timed structure.

⏱️ Time Plan (Max 10 min)

  • Plan – 2 min
  • Write – 7 min
  • Review – 1 min

📌 Suggested Structure (Mini PEEL x3)

  • Point 1: Clear idea + definition
  • Explain: Link to question + detail
  • Example: Use a specific case or stat
  • Evaluate: Mini judgement (optional)

Repeat x2 more for Point 2 and Point 3

✅ Checklist

  • [ ] 3 clear points included
  • [ ] At least 1 named example or stat
  • [ ] Used keywords from the question
  • [ ] Stayed focused on the question
  • [ ] Optional mini conclusion added

🧠 Example Question

"Assess the importance of precipitation and evapotranspiration in the water cycle." (9 marks)

🧱 Psychology 8-Marker Answer Planner

Goal: Write a clear, well-balanced 8-marker with focus on AO1 (description) and AO3 (evaluation) skills.

⏱️ Time Plan (Max 10 min)

  • Plan – 2 min
  • Write – 7 min
  • Check – 1 min

📌 Suggested Structure

  1. AO1: Brief explanation/description of key theory, model or study
  2. AO3: First evaluation point (strength or limitation)
  3. AO3: Second evaluation point (contrast or develop)
  4. Mini conclusion: (optional) Quick summary/judgement

✅ Checklist

  • [ ] Clear definition or explanation (AO1)
  • [ ] Two AO3 points (evaluation)
  • [ ] Used a study/example to support
  • [ ] Answer links to the question throughout
  • [ ] Mini conclusion or final judgement if time

🧠 Example Question

"Outline and evaluate the cognitive approach in psychology." (8 marks)
